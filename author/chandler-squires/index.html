Chandler Squires

Graduate Student

MIT

I am a fourth-year PhD student in EECS at MIT. My research is centered on learning the effects of interventions and distribution shift in complex systems, with a particular focus on cellular biology and healthcare. This spans causal structure learning, experimental design, causal representation learning, and treatment effect estimation. I am lucky to be co-advised by Caroline Uhler and David Sontag.

Slides from my talks can be found here. Lecture notes and recordings from my course on causality can be found here.

I am the main developer of causaldag.
